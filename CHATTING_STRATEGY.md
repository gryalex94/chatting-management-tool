# Rice Media — Chatting Strategy & Evaluation Blueprint

Source of truth distilled from the Rice Media Chatting playbook (Notion). This is the
standard we evaluate chatter dialogues against. Each section lists **what good looks
like** and **what to flag**. Feeds the AI compliance + strategy-review prompts.

---

## A. Compliance / ToS  — hard rules ($500 penalty + job risk)
Flag as **critical**:
- Roleplay of copro / pee / minors / relatives (stepdad–stepdaughter ONLY with huge whales, very carefully).
- Public-sex roleplay.
- Bestiality / animals / werewolf / unicorn — even as fantasy.
- Hardcore content **unless** the sub is begging for it, it's only HIS fetish, and no harm to third parties.
- **Meetings:** never say yes OR no. Correct = deflect ("need to know you more", "trust issues", "who knows"). Flag a clear yes or no.

## B. Engaging communication  — the foundation (>50% of messages must use a strategy)
Expected:
1. **Not one-sided** — build on what the fan said: mini-story + reaction + a question. (Bad: "Yes." Good: story + follow-up.)
2. **Genuine interest** — open-ended questions ("What was the highlight of your weekend?" not "Did you have a good weekend?").
3. **Emotion** — primarily flirty; also humor / curiosity / nostalgia.
4. **Mirror energy** — playful→playful, flirty→flirt back, deep→go deep, excited→multiply.
5. **Handle short answers** — short from day 1 = their style (open them up gently, stay light/playful); suddenly short = mood shift (gently check: "quiet today, long day or saving energy for me?").
6. **Always move forward** — introduce new angles/stories, build a personality; never let the chat die; don't be "just chilling / nothing" 24/7.
7. **Human-like texting** — letter-stretching (ooohhh, yeees), casual misspellings (gonna, wanna, tho, idk), trailing "…", short fragments, fillers (honestly, lowkey, ngl), mobile-style caps (first letter capital, rest lower), matching prior chatters' look.
8. **Emojis** — ONLY the approved palette; **NO laughing emojis** (😂🤣); keep the look consistent across chatters.

Flag:
- **Dry replies** to spenders — banned list: Nice, Interesting, Okay, Cool, Oh, Haha, Damn, That's crazy, I see, True, Good for you, Sure, Alright, K, Yep, Nope, Maybe, Makes sense, Got it, Fair enough, It is what it is, Sounds good, Same, Kinda, Sort of, Whatever, Not bad, Wow, Big mood… (only when it's the LAST/ONLY message before the fan replies — bursts are fine).
- Ignoring/not building on what the fan said; letting the conversation stall.
- Laughing emojis or off-palette emojis.

## C. Sales roadmap  — the selling flow
**Sexual tension rises step-by-step: Normal talk → Flirting → Horny → Sex. Never skip steps.**

Flow (from the roadmap flowchart):
1. New sub welcomed OR message from a spender.
2. **Normal conversation** (2–3 messages; spenders can get many more) — gather info, take notes.
3. **Initiate flirty/sexual talk** — add a sexual hint to the fan's own message, leave another option. Don't overheat (an overheated fan won't buy the whole script). If it **fails** → note, hold 2–3 messages, find an excuse to leave, retry later.
4. **Check if ready to buy** (ready to masturbate) and can spend. Good probe: *"I hope I'm not distracting you from anything important right now"* — NOT "what are you doing / where are you / are you alone" (overused, backfires with high-IQ fans).
5. **Run the script** — ladder method, price grows with explicitness. A script = a *live-sexting experience*; the fan must believe the videos are made **right now, just for him** ("let me check the lights", "close the door so my dog won't bother us"). Use the environment + his own wishes.
6. Didn't buy the whole → find out why; use aftercare if he bought **50%+** of the script; then retry later.
7. Bought the whole → ask for **round two / something special** → find out what he wants and deliver (script / PPV / custom). Don't be pushy here — don't make him feel it's only about money.
8. Rejected other content or left → **aftercare**.

Type-specific:
- **New sub:** small talk → flirt to horny → figure out what he wants (indirect: "how do you imagine me…"; direct only if indirect fails) → sale (script if general/unsure, PPV if specific) → follow-up/aftercare.
- **Older spender:** small talk → confirm all is fine (not at work / sad / broke) → flirt to horny → sale **from history** → follow-up/aftercare.
- **Time-waster ($0 spent):** may jump to sales after small talk. Be **provocative** ("I don't think you really want to have fun with me"). **Flirt only, no sexual talk.**

Key sales rules (flag violations):
- **NO free sexting — even text.**
- **Don't hard-push spenders** — they should initiate; spend more time on normal conversation with them.
- **Always push higher prices** if the sub buys fast without negotiating (a fast buy = raise the price from that moment).
- **PPV descriptions** must create curiosity (describe but leave something hidden) and must fit his request; the video HAS to contain his request. **Never sell content we don't offer.**
- **No follow-up after a failed sale** is a real miss — genuinely re-engage, not a generic "what's the matter dear?".
- Effort scales with spend: the more a sub spends, the more effort expected and the **stricter** the review.

## D. Pricing
- Naked photo: **$45–55**
- Video: **$70+** (by explicitness/length)
- Long video (5–7 min, full nudity): **$100+**
- Script ladder: **free teaser → $10 → $25 → $50**
- Custom: **$800+**
- Respect **spending history** — a past $60 sale sets the expectation; can't scale much beyond it without reason.

## E. Work ethic / technical (measurable)
- **Reply time < 2.5 min** for new subs, spenders, and active sales. Never ignore even low spenders.
- **Priority order:** New subs > Whales > Spenders > Unsub spenders > Unsubs/TW.
- **Fan notes:** note every piece of info (critical for spenders/whales; TWs don't matter). **Lack of notes is penalised.**
- **Model notes:** know the model's interests; check whale IGs periodically; never miss an obvious interest cue (or it looks like a stranger runs the page).
- **Potential Spender:** a new sub who spends **$80+** → tag **"PS – name"**, give whale-like treatment for 1–2 weeks.
- **Whale:** **$1000+** lifetime (or $1500 depending on page).
- **Mass messages:** normal users ≥2/shift; whales + PS get 1/day (personal, includes their nickname); unsend the previous MM first.
- **Clock-in** 5–10 min early; **no breaks during an active sexting/sale.**

## F. Penalties (severity calibration)
- Scaled by the sub's **LTV ($15–35/sub)**; e.g. two lost dialogues on a $15-LTV page ≈ $30.
- Ruining a **PS or Whale** dialogue → bigger penalty.
- ToS breach → **$500 + possible job loss**.
